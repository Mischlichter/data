P02:

P03:
To awaken the HogeAI Bot from its digital slumber, simply type /start. Quick tip: Make sure you're whispering (or, well, typing) to the right bot. If you ever need a guiding star, /help has got your back, laying out all the commands you'll need.

P04:
From Words to Hoge-tastic Visuals - With the hai: command, your words are the paint, and HogeAI is the canvas. And if you want our star, Hoge, in the scene, just drop his name in your tale.

P05:
Example - hai: Hoge is walking in a tropical garden with colorful flowers

P06:
Unleashing The Wild(side) With Wildcards - Dive into the fun of HogeAI's wildcard feature, which acts like a roll of the dice in your storytelling. Wildcards are dynamic placeholders that can vary from simple elements, styles, to  fully fleshed-out prompts. By using the /wildcard command, you can access our curated list. When you include a wildcard in your prompt, HogeAI randomly selects from the specified category, adding a unique and unpredictable twist to your creation. This feature enriches your narratives, allowing for spontaneous and creative results that surprise and delight.

P07:
Example - hai: !emoji! Hoge in a !art! style from !nations!

SCENE04:

P01:
PROMPT GUIDE

P02:
Setting the Stage - Alright, adventurer! Before you unleash your creativity, let's lay down some ground rules. Crafting the perfect prompt is like whipping up a magical potion: a dash of description, a sprinkle of specifics, and a big ol' dollop of imagination.

P03:
The Basics - Be descriptive: The more you tell, the more we show. Instead of "Hoge with a hat", try "Hoge standing on a beach, wearing a cowboy hat in neon green, golden sand, grassy dunes, sunny day, peaceful sounds of nature, 8K UHD". See the difference? Set the mood and setting: Is it a sunny day at the beach or a mysterious moonlit forest? Your choice, your story.

P04:
Dive Deeper - Colors & Textures: Add some flair! "Hoge's silky blue scarf" paints a clearer picture than just "Hoge's scarf". Actions & Emotions: What's Hoge up to? Is he dancing, meditating, or maybe feeling ecstatic or pensive? Let us know!

P05:
Pro Tips - Keep It Clear: While details are awesome, don't overload. "Hoge having a picnic with unicorns, dragons, and robots on Mars while a meteor shower is happening" might just leave the AI scratching its digital head. Use wildcards for an element of surprise. If you're ever feeling indecisive, let the wildcard decide!

P06:
Advanced Prompting Tactics - Order Matters: Start with the main idea, then sprinkle in the details. Words at the beginning carry more weight. Ditch "very big". Opt for "gigantic" or "colossal". Aim for a blueprint that outlines the Main Subject, Environment/Setting, and Style/Mood.

P07:
Technical Mastery - Lighting & Camera: Lighting and camera angles can transform your image. Try "soft ambient lighting for calm scenes," "cinematic lighting for theater vibes," or "neon lights for a futuristic feel." Pair with angles like "low-angle for hero shots," "aerial for sweeping views," or "close-up for details." Experiment with techniques like "watercolor washes for a soft look," "oil painting for rich textures," or "pencil sketch for fine lines." Mix and match to perfect your image's mood and quality.

P08:
Using References - Art and Media Influences: Mentioning specific references can guide the AI effectively. If you want a scene reminiscent of a popular movie or artwork, include that in your prompt. For example, "inspired by the cyberpunk aesthetic of Blade Runner" can yield highly stylized and recognizable imagery.

P09:
Experiment & Explore - The beauty of HogeAI? There's no right or wrong. Play around and most importantly, have a blast! From serene landscapes to wacky adventures, the Hoge universe is yours to explore.
